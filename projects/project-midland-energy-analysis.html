Midland Energy Resources - Cost of Capital Analysis

Project Overview

This corporate finance project analyzed and calculated the Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) for multiple divisions at Midland Energy Resources. Recommendations were provided on capital structure, debt capacity, and investment decisions based on the analysis.

Tools & Techniques

  • WACC Calculation for multiple divisions
  • Capital Structure and Debt Capacity Analysis
  • CAPM for risk management
  • Excel and Bloomberg Terminal for financial analysis
