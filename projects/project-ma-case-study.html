M&A Case Study: Spotify-Peloton Merger

Project Overview

This case study examined the potential merger between Spotify and Peloton using Bloomberg Terminal and financial models to assess the strategic and financial fit between the two companies.

Tools & Techniques

  • Bloomberg Terminal for financial data
  • Accretion/Dilution Models
  • Financial Forecasts
  • Company Valuations and Synergy Analysis

Results

The analysis highlighted the strategic benefits of the merger, including increased market reach and operational synergies. Financial forecasts predicted positive accretion, supporting the merger decision.
